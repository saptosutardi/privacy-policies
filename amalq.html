Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the "AmalQ: Ladang Amal" application handles data. Our application is designed as a personal habit and worship tracking tool that stores all data locally on your device. We are committed to maintaining your privacy and ensuring that no personal information is collected, stored on remote servers, or transmitted to third parties.

1. Local Data Storage

AmalQ stores data locally on your device using SharedPreferences (mobile) and local storage mechanisms. The application collects and stores only the following information exclusively on your device:

  • Amal (Good Deeds) Records: Names, icons, colors, target days, and active status of habits you create
  • Daily Completion Records: Which habits were completed on specific dates and their completion timestamps
  • User Preferences: Reset time, theme mode preference (light/dark/system), statistics display settings, and week configuration
  • Onboarding Status: Whether you have viewed the introductory guides

Importantly: We do NOT collect personal identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or any demographic data.

2. No Remote Data Transmission

This application does not transmit, sync, or upload your data to any remote servers, cloud services, or external servers. All data remains entirely on your device. Your habit data is not shared with us, backup services, or any third party.

3. Data Security

Because the application is designed to store data locally and does not transmit information to external servers, the risk of data interception or breach through network transmission is eliminated. However, the security of data stored on your device depends on your device's security settings and screen lock protection. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device locked with a strong PIN or biometric authentication
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Not sharing your device with untrusted individuals

4. Third-Party Services

This application does not integrate with or use any third-party services for data collection or tracking, including:

  • Advertising networks or ad tracking services
  • Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Cloud storage services (Google Drive, iCloud, Dropbox)
  • Crash reporting systems
  • Social media integrations
  • User tracking or profiling services

5. Permissions

AmalQ requests only minimal permissions necessary for basic operation:

  • Access to device storage for reading and writing local data files
  • System clock access for date/time functions

The app does not request access to contacts, location, camera, microphone, calendar, or any other sensitive data. If your device asks for these permissions, please deny them.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device for as long as the application remains installed. You have complete control over your data:

  • Individual Deletion: You can delete individual habits or records within the app
  • Complete Deletion: Uninstalling the application will remove all associated data from your device
  • Manual Export: Your data is stored in plain JSON format and can be manually exported if you choose

We retain no copies of your data on our servers or elsewhere.

7. Children's Privacy

AmalQ can be used by individuals of any age, including minors. Since the application does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information—including names, ages, or any identifying data—it complies with children's privacy regulations such as COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and other international child protection laws. Parents and guardians can safely allow children to use this application.

8. International Compliance

This application complies with major global privacy regulations, including:

  • GDPR (European Union): Since no personal data is transmitted to external servers, GDPR requirements are satisfied through local-only storage.
  • CCPA (California, USA): No personal data is collected, sold, or shared.
  • PIPEDA (Canada): No personal information is processed or transmitted.
  • UU PDP 2022 (Indonesia): No personal data is processed or transmitted outside personal device storage.
  • LGPD (Brazil): No personal data is collected or shared.

10. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device and not on our servers, you have complete control and ownership of your information:

  • Right to Access: All your data is accessible to you directly through the application
  • Right to Modify: You can edit or update any of your habits and records at any time
  • Right to Delete: You can delete individual items or all data by uninstalling the application
  • Right to Portability: Your data is stored in a standard format (JSON) that you can access
