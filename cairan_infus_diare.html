Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the “Pediatric Diarrhea Fluid” application handles data. Our application is designed as a fully offline tool that performs all calculations directly on your device. We are committed to maintaining your privacy and ensuring that no personal or medical information is collected, stored, or transmitted.

1. No Data Collection

The application does not collect, record, store, or transmit any type of data. All information entered into the app—including patient weight, dehydration status, or any other medical inputs—is processed solely on your device and not shared with us or any third party.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Patient or medical data
  • Device identifiers, IP address, or location
  • Analytics or usage activity
  • Any information entered into the application

2. Data Security

Because the application is fully offline and does not transmit data to external servers, the risk of data interception or breach is eliminated. Any information entered is temporary and remains only in your device’s memory during the active session.

3. Third-Party Services

This application does not use or integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Cloud storage
  • Crash reporting systems
  • Social media integrations

4. Permissions

The app does not request access to contacts, location, camera, microphone, storage, or any other personal data. Only minimal permissions required by the operating system to run the app are used.

5. Children's Privacy

Important: This app is intended for healthcare professionals, medical trainees, and caregivers. While it is used for pediatric medical calculations, it does not collect any data about children or any other individuals.

We comply with COPPA and other international child privacy regulations. Since no data is collected, there is no risk of children's information being captured or stored.

6. Medical Disclaimer

This application is a clinical decision-support tool and is not a substitute for professional medical judgment. Healthcare providers remain fully responsible for all clinical decisions.

The app does not diagnose, treat, or store medical records. It only provides calculation assistance based on user input.

7. International Compliance

This application complies with major global privacy regulations, including:

  • GDPR (EU): No personal data is collected.
  • CCPA (California): No data is sold or shared.
  • HIPAA (USA): The app does not store, process, or transmit Protected Health Information (PHI).
  • UU PDP 2022 (Indonesia): No personal data is processed.

9. Data Retention

We retain no data. All user inputs are temporary and cleared when you exit or reset the application.

10. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store personal data, there is no data to access, modify, delete, or export. You maintain full control over the information you input, which remains solely on your device.
