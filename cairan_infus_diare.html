Privacy Policy for Pediatric Diarrhea Fluid

Effective Date: November 28, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy outlines our practices regarding data for the "Pediatric Diarrhea Fluid" application. Our core principle is to respect your privacy, and as such, our application is designed to be a completely offline tool.

1. No Data Collection

The "Pediatric Diarrhea Fluid" application is a standalone, offline tool. We do not collect, record, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information or any other data you enter into the application (such as patient weight, age, dehydration status, etc.). All calculations and data processing occur exclusively on your local device.

2. Data Security

Since the application does not collect or store any data, there is no risk of your data being compromised or breached through the app. Any information you input is temporary and is discarded once you close the application.

3. Permissions

The application does not require any special device permissions (like access to contacts, location, or storage) to function.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
